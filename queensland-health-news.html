Infrastructure October 12, 2023

New $250 Million Hospital Wing Opens in Brisbane

The Queensland Government has officially opened the new state-of-the-art clinical services building at the Royal Brisbane and Women's Hospital. The facility features 150 new beds, expanded emergency services, and specialized treatment areas.

Health Minister Shannon Fentiman said the new wing would significantly increase capacity and reduce wait times for patients across southeast Queensland.

Public Health October 8, 2023

Seasonal Vaccination Campaign Launches Across Queensland

Queensland Health has launched its annual influenza vaccination campaign, with free vaccines now available for eligible groups including seniors, pregnant women, and people with chronic health conditions.

Chief Health Officer Dr John Gerrard urged Queenslanders to get vaccinated ahead of the peak flu season, noting that vaccination rates have declined since the COVID-19 pandemic.

Mental Health October 3, 2023

New Mental Health Support Service for Regional Queensland

A new telehealth mental health service has been launched to provide better access to psychological support for people in rural and remote areas of Queensland.

The service will connect patients with specialists via video consultation, reducing the need for long-distance travel and wait times for mental health care.

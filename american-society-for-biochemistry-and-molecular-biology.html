Our Mission

The American Society for Biochemistry and Molecular Biology (ASBMB) is an international nonprofit scientific and educational organization. With over 11,000 members, the society advances scientific discovery through its journals, scientific meetings, advocacy and education programs.

Founded in 1906, ASBMB is one of the oldest and most respected molecular life science societies. Our members work in academia, industry, and government to further our understanding of the molecular nature of life processes.

ASBMB publishes three highly cited peer-reviewed journals, organizes scientific meetings, advocates for funding of research, provides career development resources, and promotes science education at all levels.
